Welcome to DawgDex

DawgDex is a dog breed discovery app powered by TheDogAPI. Explore random breeds, search for a specific breed, view detailed stats, and save your dogs!

Browse Random Breeds

Head to the Breed Explorer to discover 6 randomly selected dog breeds pulled live from TheDogAPI.

View Breed Details

Click on any dog to see detailed stats including breed group, origin, temperament, life span, height and weight.

Save Your Favourites

Save individual dogs from the Breed Explorer to your personal saved collection.

View Saved Dogs

Browse all previously saved dogs in one place, with the option to delete any you no longer want.
